Founders Hub is a Friday morning community for startup founders and technology leaders who want sharper business support, useful introductions, and a stronger route into Scotland's technology ecosystem.

See upcoming meetups Explore member access
Why Join

A trusted room for the conversations that move companies forward.

Meet founders, operators, investors, ecosystem partners, and company representatives who understand the reality of growing a technology business in Scotland.

Each gathering is designed to turn a Friday morning into momentum: practical support, peer perspective, warm introductions, and the occasional question that unlocks the next move.

Meeting Rhythm

In person in Edinburgh on the 1st and 3rd Friday morning of each month.

Start the day with people who can open doors, compare notes, and help you navigate the next stage of your business.
